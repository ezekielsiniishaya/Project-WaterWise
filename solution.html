Our Solution:

In order to solve the challenges related to water conservation and recycling here are few proposed solutions:

  1. Green Infrastructure:
      2. The best strategy to manage flood water, urban heat effects, and promote sustainability is green infrastructure that provides an alternative to traditional gray concrete infrastructure by making hard/paved surfaces vegetated and permeable. When installed at large scale like green roofs or permeable pavements capture rain water and retain it on site regulates the temperature, provides wildlife habitat, and reduces greenhouse gas reduction benefits.
  2. Precision irrigation system for water conservation:
      3. Drip irrigation: System that deliver water directly to the root zone of plants, minimizing evaporation and run off. For this precise farming use of soil and ambient sensors and controllers should be incorporated to precisely regulate irrigation schedules. We have created a prototype as our final year project: Cyber pysical system for precision farming ensuring water conservation and precision farming.

  3. Investing in Renewable Energy:
      4. Transitioning to renewable energy sources help reduce greenhouse gas emmisions contributing to climate change, incorporating renewable energy solutions, solar powered water pumps, similarly wind powered desalination plants as world is already moving towards zero carbon focusing on sustainabilty and clean energy.
  4. Industrial waste water treatment techniques:
      5. The most efficient technique for industrial water treatment and pollution reduction depends on various factors, including the specific contaminants present in the wastewater, the volume and flow rate of wastewater, and economic considerations.
      • Advanced Filtration Technologies:
        Effective for removing a wide range of contaminants, including suspended solids, dissolved solids, and microorganisms. Suitable for industries with complex wastewater compositions requiring high-purity water treatment.
